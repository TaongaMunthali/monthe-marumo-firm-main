Monthe Marumo & Co was established through the merger of Monthe Marumo & Company and Molatlhegi & Associates.



The firm’s roots trace back to 1983 when it was founded by former Chief Justice J. M. Nganunu. In 1992, Justice Nganunu was appointed to the High Court, paving the way for Managing Partner K. N. Monthe to team up with Justice A. B. Tafa to form the successful Tafa Monthe & Co legal practice. When Justice Tafa became Deputy Attorney General, Mr. Monthe took the helm.



On April 1, 1992, M. B. Marumo joined forces with Mr. Monthe, officially forming Monthe Marumo & Co. The firm grew further in 2000 with the addition of B. D. Leburu as a partner. A year later, Mr. Marumo was appointed to the High Court of Botswana, continuing the legacy of excellence associated with the firm.

Today, Monthe Marumo & Co is a leading name in Botswana’s legal landscape, offering expertise across diverse legal domains, including:

Commercial Law, Administrative Law, General & Specialist Litigation, Arbitration & Mediation, Corporate Law, Property & Land Law, Contract & Construction Law.

The firm also handles matters in Electoral Law, Human Rights Law, Family Law, Delictual Claims, and Labour & Employment Law, making it a versatile and trusted partner for a wide range of legal needs.
