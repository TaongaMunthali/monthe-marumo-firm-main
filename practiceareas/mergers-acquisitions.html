Our firm offers comprehensive legal services in property law, tailored to meet the diverse needs of individuals, businesses, and developers in Botswana. With a deep understanding of the local property market and regulatory framework, we provide strategic advice and practical solutions for all aspects of property transactions, development, and management.

Advisory Services and Lease Drafting

We provide expert guidance on the drafting and negotiation of commercial and residential leases. Our goal is to ensure that lease agreements are structured to protect our clients’ interests while complying with Botswana’s legal standards. Whether you are a landlord or tenant, we assist in creating robust agreements that minimize disputes and promote successful tenancy relationships.

Development Projects and Township Establishments

Our team has extensive experience in handling complex commercial and residential development projects, including:

  • Township Establishment: Guiding developers through the intricate process of creating townships, ensuring compliance with zoning and regulatory requirements.
  • Sectional Title Developments: Advising on the structuring and registration of sectional title schemes, enabling clients to maximize the value of their developments.

Property Transfers

We handle all types of property transfers with precision and efficiency, including:

  • Conventional transfers of properties.
  • Transfers arising from liquidations, auctions, court orders, foreclosures, divorces, donations, deceased estates, and insolvent estates. Our expertise ensures a seamless conveyancing process, even in complex or high-stakes transactions.

Financial Structuring and Planning

We provide innovative solutions for the financial structuring of property transactions,

  • Share Block and Time Share Schemes: Advising on the financial and legal frameworks for these specialized property ownership models.
  • Project Financing: Offering guidance on structuring finance for large-scale property developments and commercial projects.

Conveyancing Applications and Consents

Navigating the conveyancing process often requires various consents, endorsements, and applications. Our team manages these requirements efficiently, ensuring all necessary documentation is secured to facilitate smooth transactions.

Agreements for Sale of Property

We specialize in negotiating and drafting agreements for the sale of both residential and commercial properties. Our contracts are designed to safeguard our clients’ interests while meeting all legal and regulatory requirements.

Real Estate Finance and Bonds

We offer a full suite of services related to the registration and cancellation of bonds,

  • Mortgage Bonds
  • Surety Bonds
  • Collateral Bonds
  • Notarial Bonds

Our team ensures that bond registrations are handled accurately and promptly to support clients’ financial and transactional goals.

Servitudes and Property Modifications

We assist in the registration of all types of servitudes, as well as the subdivision and consolidation of properties. Whether you are modifying property boundaries or creating rights of way, our team ensures the process is completed in accordance with Botswana’s property laws.

Environmental, Safety, and Planning Compliance

Property development and management require strict adherence to environmental and safety regulations. We provide guidance on planning approvals and compliance to help clients avoid legal and operational setbacks.

Real Estate Development and Investment

From concept to completion, we support real estate developers with legal advice and strategic planning for:

  • Residential and commercial developments.
  • Investment opportunities in Botswana’s growing real estate market.

Real Estate Disputes

When disputes arise, our team is equipped to handle litigation and alternative dispute resolution processes. We advocate for our clients in matters such as lease disputes, boundary disagreements, and breaches of sale agreements.
