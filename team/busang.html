Busang D Leburu

Mr. Busang Dutch Leburu is a seasoned lawyer with over 21 years of practical legal experience in private practice. He holds a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) degree from the University of Botswana and is currently pursuing a Master of Laws (LLM) at the same institution, specializing in International Trade Law and Labour Relations.

Mr. Leburu’s expertise spans several key legal areas, including: • Labour Law • Land Law and Policy • Commercial and Corporate Law (including Competition Law and Financial Law) • Contract Negotiations and Drafting • Procurement Law and Advisory (including Venture Capital and legal aspects of outsourcing and international trade).

IHe has also served as a member of the Independent Complaints Review Committee, where he adjudicated appeals from the National Procurement Body (PPADB), further showcasing his depth of knowledge in procurement and dispute resolution.

Mr. Leburu’s extensive experience and specialization make him a trusted authority in corporate legal matters, policy advisory, and international trade law.
