Dr Baalthodi Molatlhegi

Dr. Baatlhodi Molatlhegi is a founding and senior partner at Molatlhegi & Associates, a leading commercial and regulatory consulting law firm based in Gaborone, Botswana. With over 23 years of practical legal experience spanning academia, the public sector, and private practice, Dr. Molatlhegi is widely regarded as Botswana’s foremost regulatory attorney and is highly recognized across the SADC region.

His expertise lies in: • Regulatory Law and Policy • Legislative Review and Drafting • Legal Aspects of Institutional Design • Design of Compliance Matrices for private and parastatal entities • International Trade

Dr. Molatlhegi holds a doctoral degree focused on regional integration within the SADC region, which underscores his authority in cross-border legal and regulatory frameworks. He is also well-versed in commercial law, including: • Licensing • Contract Negotiations • Procurement Processes, including Public-Private Partnerships (PPP)

Dr. Molatlhegi has successfully undertaken legal and regulatory consulting assignments both within Botswana and across the SADC region and beyond. He provides expert advice on corporate governance and has played a pivotal role in setting up new statutory organizations.

His leadership credentials include serving as a Director at Botswana Savings Bank and as Board Secretary of the Botswana Telecommunications Authority, where he gained extensive experience in corporate governance and institutional design. Dr. Molatlhegi’s unique blend of regulatory, commercial, and governance expertise positions him as a trusted advisor and leader in the legal and regulatory space.
